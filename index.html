A multimedia message from Jeff Bezos

A Message from the Past

In this speculative media artifact, Jeff Bezos addresses the Amazon shareholders of 2075 through a multimedia letter created in 2025—fifty years before its intended release. Blending video, sound, and interface design, this fictional time capsule examines legacy, power, and the techno-utopian dreams of one of the 21st century’s most influential figures. What happens when a corporate vision is engineered to outlive its creator?
